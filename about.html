About Plantorium🌿

Among the quiet paths and the hum of leaves, lies a small effort to connect nature and technology. This website is a digital greenhouse — a living archive of the plants that thrive within the M.C.G.M Garden - Kamalakarpant Walawalkar Udyan, Oshiwara.

“Every leaf speaks bliss to me, fluttering from the autumn tree.”
– Emily Brontë

🌱 Our Purpose

Each plant within this garden carries its own story — scientific, cultural, and spiritual. Our aim is to preserve and share this knowledge in the simplest way possible. By scanning the QR codes hung near every plant, visitors can instantly access detailed information about its origin, growth pattern, medicinal use, and unique characteristics.

🍃 The Process

The data for each plant was collected manually through field visits — noting their scientific names, capturing high-quality images, and verifying their taxonomy. All information was then integrated into this platform for easy access and learning.

🌸 Vision

This project hopes to inspire curiosity, mindfulness, and respect for the green life around us. Whether you are a student, researcher, or just a passerby who paused to admire a leaf — we welcome you into this little digital forest.
